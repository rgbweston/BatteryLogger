# Privacy Policy — Battery Logger

**Last updated: 3 April 2026**

## What data is collected

When the Battery Logger widget is running, the following data is recorded at regular intervals and sent to a server operated by the app developer:

- Battery percentage
- Charging status (charging or not charging)
- A device identifier generated by the Garmin Connect IQ platform (unique per app, not linked to your Garmin account)
- Device model (part number)
- Firmware version
- Timestamp of each reading
- App version

## How data is used

Data is used solely to track battery level over time for personal analysis. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

## Data sharing

Data is not shared with any third parties.

## Data storage

Readings are stored on a server operated by the app developer. Data is retained indefinitely unless a deletion request is made.
